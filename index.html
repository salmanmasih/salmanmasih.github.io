About Me

I’m Salman Masih, a Ph.D. researcher in Natural Language Processing focusing on multilingual models, legal text summarization, and LLM fairness for low-resource languages like Urdu.

Research Interests

My research interests include multilingual NLP, legal text summarization, hallucination mitigation, and cross-lingual fairness. I focus on developing effective techniques for low-resource languages, particularly Urdu, and enhancing the efficiency of large language models (LLMs) in these contexts.
