Bio

Salman Masih is an avid academic with profound appreciation of the Computer Science and particularly one of its emerging area i.e. data science. He has gone through a comprehensive training ranging from basic statistics to the advanced mathematical modeling for machine learning and recently completed a specialization namely “Data Science with Python Track “from “Data-Camp” which comprises of twenty-two courses with numerous exercises, small projects and real-life data analysis. Prior to assuming the current position, he had worked with University of Sialkot for four years as lecturer. He has been teaching several courses at undergrad level such as Programming Fundamentals, Object Oriented Programing, Design and Analysis of Algorithms, Artificial Intelligence, Data Science, Statistics & Probability and Compiler Construction. Moreover, coursera.org, edx.org, and udacity.com are other homes for him due to his never lasting desire to learn new courses and he enjoys English sitcoms to boost his vocabulary.
